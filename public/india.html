Scoring Methodology Deep Dive

GDP-Adjusted WTP

Economic capacity assessment goes beyond raw GDP figures to estimate realistic willingness-to-pay for SaaS products, accounting for price sensitivity variations across markets.

Key Insight: Markets with higher English proficiency show stronger correlation with SaaS adoption willingness, creating a multiplier effect on GDP-based estimates.

Competitive Landscape

Evaluation of local competitive intensity considers presence of global no-code platforms (Bubble, Softr, Retool) and density of local development agencies.

Pricing Research Methodology

Van Westendorp PSM

Gabor-Granger Method

Direct purchase intent assessment at varying price points to construct demand curves and identify revenue-optimizing pricing.

Key Variables Tested: Seat-based vs. usage-based models, local currency vs. USD pricing, annual vs. monthly billing cycles.
